SYS.003 — RESEARCH LOG

Two research papers currently in progress.

UniParaAI

In Progress

Unified Parallel LLM Framework for Autonomous Vehicles & UAV Swarms

  • Applying MPI tensor parallelism to distribute LLM inference across 4–8 nodes, targeting a ≥2x reduction in latency for real-time autonomous vehicle and UAV control.
  • Designing a CNN Adversarial Input Guard — an unsupervised convolutional autoencoder — to detect 7 documented sensor attack vectors (RGB, LiDAR, GPS/IMU, DVS) at ≥95% accuracy with a false-positive rate of ≤5%.
  • Implementing adaptive load balancing alongside a MapReduce-based scenario-generation pipeline, targeting a threefold improvement over sequential baselines.

Cyberattack Resilience of AV Sensor Systems

Reproducibility Study

Reproducing EfficientAD student–teacher anomaly detection for autonomous vehicle sensors

  • Reproduced the EfficientAD pipeline to detect seven cyberattack types across RGB and DVS sensor modalities.
  • Achieved 100% detection accuracy on three RGB attack types, with 83.1% overall RGB accuracy, an AUROC of 0.903, and an F1 score of 89.4%.
  • Conducted the full study under a hardware-constrained 0.2 GB VRAM budget, using rigorous synthetic data generation methods.
